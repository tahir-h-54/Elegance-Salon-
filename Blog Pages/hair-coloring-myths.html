Hair Coloring Myths You Should Stop Believing

Many clients worry that “all color damages hair,” but modern techniques and professional products make coloring safer than ever. You can go lighter gradually without harming your hair when done correctly. Using color-safe shampoo and regular treatments keeps your shade fresh and healthy

Another common myth is that once you color your hair, you can’t go back to your natural tone. With expert color correction, transitioning shades is completely possible and can be done gently.

At Elegance Salon, we focus on hair health first — offering personalized color options that enhance your beauty while keeping your hair soft, vibrant, and strong.
