How to Prepare for Your First Keratin Treatment

Keratin treatments are perfect for smooth, frizz-free, shiny hair — but prep matters! Before your appointment, avoid heavy conditioners or oil treatments for 48 hours, and come in with clean, dry hair. After your session, keep your hair dry and avoid tying it back for the recommended period.

Keratin strengthens your hair from within, making styling easier and reducing heat damage. With proper care, results can last several months and keep your hair silky and manageable.

Our team at Elegance Salon will guide you through the process, recommend the right aftercare products, and make sure your keratin treatment delivers long-lasting shine and smoothness.
